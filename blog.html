Fighting Against Corruption: A Call to Moral Integrity

Corruption undermines human dignity and erodes public trust. CJPD works to foster a culture of honesty, integrity, and accountability through civic education and advocacy. Christians are encouraged to live by truth, reject bribery, and be role models of ethical leadership in society.

Protecting Our Children: Ending Abuse Through Awareness

CJPD partners with parishes, schools, and social institutions to raise awareness on child safety and report abuse cases. We believe every child deserves protection, love, and a safe environment to grow in faith and dignity.
